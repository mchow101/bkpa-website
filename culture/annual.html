Performances and Events

BKPA hosts annual cultural events, including Saraswati Puja, Kali Puja, and Bengali New Year.

Our cultural events feature performances by member students and guest artists as well as food, games, and short religious ceremonies for religious holidays.

We welcome guests of any background to join us at these events.

In addition to events we host, our past highlights include performing at North American Bengali Conference (NABC) 2010 in Atlantic City, Milan Mela 2012 and 2014, UTSOV's Durga Pujas, Princeton Public Library 2015, the Asian American Heritage Festival 2016, and Consulate General of NY 2019.
