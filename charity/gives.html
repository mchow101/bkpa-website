BKPA Gives

BKPA Gives is the charitable branch of BKPA. We donate food, clothes, shoes, technology, and funds to individuals and families in need in America, India, Bangladesh, and around the world through our clothing drives, food drives, and International Technology for Self Sufficiency programs.

Through these programs we assist individuals and families with immediate obstacles and help them create and maintain sustainable careers. BKPA Gives also oversees our fundraising partnerships with organizations such as the Poets Foundation, organizes participation in group events such as the March of Dimes, and puts on free performances in assisted living facilities.
