About BKPA

BKPA has four pillars around which we structure our activities: Educational, Charitable, Literary, and Cultural. We host events, classes, fundraisers, and more to support goals in each of these areas.

As a volunteer-run organization for kids, our programs are generated by and focused on kids.

BKPA was founded in the early 2000s by Anita Dam. Anita was inspired to create a community where she could learn about, celebrate, and share her heritage and connect with other Bengali and Bengali-American kids. This drive to forge cultural connections has led to BKPA blossoming into an international community.
